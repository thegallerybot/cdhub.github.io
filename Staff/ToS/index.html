Staff Terms of Service

These terms outline the responsibilities and guidelines for CD Hub staff members.

1. General Conduct

All staff members are expected to behave professionally and adhere to ethical standards while representing CD Hub.

1.1. Confidentiality

Staff must maintain confidentiality of sensitive information, including user data, internal communications, and project details.

1.2. Communication

Prompt and respectful communication with users and team members is mandatory. Misuse of position will result in removal.

2. Responsibilities

Staff are expected to fulfill their assigned roles diligently. This includes timely updates, responding to user queries, and assisting in ongoing tasks.

2.1. Availability

Staff members should provide their availability schedule and inform their team lead in case of extended absences.

2.2. Role Execution

Every staff member must adhere to their defined roles, assisting users and maintaining the quality of CD Hub services.

3. Reporting Issues

Staff members must report any observed issues, misconduct, or violations of terms to the appropriate authority promptly.

3.1. User Violations

Instances of user misconduct should be documented and escalated via the correct internal channels.

3.2. Internal Issues

Concerns or grievances within the team must be reported for resolution, ensuring a collaborative work environment.
