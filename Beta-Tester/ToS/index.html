Beta Tester Terms of Service

These terms outline the responsibilities and guidelines for CD Hub Beta Testers.

1. Participation Requirements

Beta Testers must meet eligibility criteria, including age requirements and agreement to follow the terms outlined.

1.1. Feedback Contribution

Participants are expected to provide constructive feedback on features, usability, and bugs.

1.2. Active Involvement

Testers should actively engage in beta testing phases, including participating in surveys or reporting issues promptly.

2. Confidentiality

Beta Testers agree to maintain confidentiality regarding unreleased features, updates, and proprietary information.

2.1. Non-Disclosure

Sharing or leaking beta content to unauthorized parties is strictly prohibited.

2.2. Internal Use Only

All provided materials and beta access are for personal testing purposes only.

3. Responsibilities

Beta Testers have specific responsibilities, including:

  • Reporting issues through designated feedback channels.
  • Completing testing within assigned timelines.
  • Respecting other participants and the community guidelines.

4. Termination of Participation

CD Hub reserves the right to remove participants from the program for failure to comply with these terms.

4.1. Breach of Terms

Violations, such as sharing confidential materials, will result in immediate removal.

4.2. Voluntary Withdrawal

Participants may opt out of the program at any time by notifying the team.
